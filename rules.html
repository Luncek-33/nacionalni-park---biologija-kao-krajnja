OFFICIAL PARK REGULATIONS

Visitor Safety, Conduct & Environmental Protection Rules

These rules ensure safety, preservation of nature, and a premium experience for all visitors of Mountain Lagoon National Park.

General Conduct

  1. Respect all park staff instructions at all times.
  2. Maintain silence in protected wildlife zones.
  3. Do not damage plants, trees, or natural formations.
  4. Littering is strictly prohibited.
  5. Smoking is allowed only in designated areas.
  6. Alcohol consumption is prohibited in nature zones.
  7. Follow all marked trails and paths.
  8. Do not enter restricted zones.
  9. Flying drones requires official permission.
  10. Commercial filming must be pre-approved.

Safety Regulations

  1. Swimming allowed only in marked lagoon zones.
  2. Life jackets are mandatory for water sports.
  3. Skiing is allowed only on designated slopes.
  4. Hiking at night requires guided supervision.
  5. Emergency signals must be reported immediately.
  6. Do not approach wild animals.
  7. Feeding wildlife is strictly forbidden.
  8. First aid stations must be used for injuries.
  9. Follow weather warnings without exception.
  10. Group separation in wilderness areas is prohibited.

Environmental Protection

  1. Do not remove natural objects from the park.
  2. Stay on designated eco-paths.
  3. No plastic waste allowed in park zones.
  4. Use refill stations for water bottles.
  5. Protect nesting and breeding areas.
  6. No loud music in natural zones.
  7. Respect seasonal restricted areas.
  8. Do not carve or mark trees or rocks.
  9. Fishing only in approved lakes.
  10. Eco-vehicles must be used where required.

Visitor Responsibility

  1. Visitors must carry valid identification.
  2. Children must be supervised at all times.
  3. Insurance is recommended for activities.
  4. Park tickets are non-transferable.
  5. Respect queue systems and booking slots.
  6. Report suspicious activity immediately.
  7. Lost items must be reported to reception.
  8. Follow evacuation instructions if needed.
  9. Do not obstruct emergency routes.
  10. Park access may be revoked for violations.

Final Regulations

  1. Park authorities reserve the right to inspect visitors' equipment.
  2. Violation of rules may result in fines or bans.
  3. Rules apply to all visitors without exception.
  4. Park management may update rules anytime.
  5. Respect for nature is mandatory at all times.
  6. Visitors must prioritize safety over convenience.
  7. Use of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
  8. Respect cultural and ecological heritage sites.
  9. Unauthorized entry into staff-only zones is forbidden.
  10. Enjoy responsibly and preserve the park for others.
