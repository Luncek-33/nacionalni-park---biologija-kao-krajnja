ABOUT THE PARK

A Natural Paradise Hidden Between Bosnia’s Majestic Mountains

Mountain Lagoon was created to preserve the extraordinary biodiversity of Bosnia and Herzegovina while offering visitors a luxury mountain experience immersed in nature.

The park is home to rare endemic plant species, protected mountain animals, panoramic walking trails, modern sport terrains, summer pools, wellness areas, and one of the region’s most attractive winter ski centers.
